**Full Description**:
MS Tools is a powerful collection of 65+ free utility tools and calculators for everyday tasks. No signup, no clutter — just fast tools that work in your browser or offline as a PWA.

Essential utilities:
• PDF tools: merge, split, compress, convert to/from images and text
• Timers: pomodoro, study timer, exam countdown, breathing exercises, meditation
• Generators: QR code, password, lorem ipsum
• Text tools: word/character counter, case converter, JSON formatter, diff checker
• Converters: unit converter, time zone converter, image compressor
• Security: password generator, strength checker

Plus calculators for health (BMR, TDEE, body fat, sleep, water intake, heart rate, running pace, pregnancy due date, caffeine & more), finance (EMI, tip, savings, loan comparison), date & time (age calculator, date difference, time calculator, countdown), and math (percentage, random decision maker, name compatibility).

Key features:
✓ 65+ free tools — all accessible without signup
✓ Offline support — works as a PWA
✓ Mobile-friendly — optimized for phones and tablets
✓ Privacy-first — no data collection
✓ Regular updates — new tools added frequently
